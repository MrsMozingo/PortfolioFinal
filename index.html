About

Whether you are seeking a minimalist or bold and dynamic website design, I can deliver on your vision. Beyond website design, my services extend to crafting distinctive logos, developing new brand identities, and managing social media marketing efforts with engaging content for your customers. By collaborating closely with you, I am dedicated to achieving your objectives and ensuring your design needs stand out in a competitive market.

Mission Statement

Whatsoever ye do, do all to the glory of God.

-1 Corinthians 10:31b

In this verse, the Apostle Paul is addressing the church in Corinth, emphasizing the importance of doing everything in a way that brings glory and honor to God. The specific context involves discussions about food sacrificed to idols and the liberties believers have in Christ. But Paul broadens the principle to encompass all aspects of life.

I believe this is a reminder that in all our actions and decisions, whether they seem spiritual or mundane, we should seek to honor and glorify God. We should live out our lives with intentionality and purpose, being mindful of how our actions reflect on our relationship with God and how they impact others around us. This can be applied to all areas of life, from the food we consume to our work, relationships, and leisure activities and even designs. The idea is to live a life that is consistently aligned with God's will and purposes for us.

While I, like everyone, stumble at times, my mission remains clear: to provide clients with digital works of art that give God the glory, regardless of the nature of the requested design.

BarterUp! App

From Concept to Reality

In the dynamic and challenging project of designing the "BarterUp!" application, I embarked on a comprehensive journey through the entire design process. My role was pivotal in transforming initial concepts into a user-centric digital solution. At the heart of my approach was a deep empathy for our users, investing time in understanding their needs and experiences to ensure our design was profoundly user-centric. A significant part of my responsibility involved pinpointing primary user challenges and pain points, analyzing feedback and behavior to identify areas for substantial improvement.

Creativity was key in the ideation and conceptualization phase, where I spearheaded brainstorming sessions to explore a multitude of design possibilities. This phase transitioned into the tangible creation of the app's design elements, including detailed wireframes, engaging mockups, and interactive prototypes. Conducting extensive usability studies was crucial, helping gauge user interaction and resonance with our designs. Feedback was invaluable, leading to iterative improvements and refinements based on user responses and study outcomes.

Book Design

Natural Alternatives: Recipes for a Healthier Home

Take Control of Your Health and Environment with Simple, Safe, and Effective DIY Solutions

Designed a book on recipes for natural alternatives in Adobe InDesign taught me the importance of layout consistency and thematic coherence. Key lessons included using master pages for uniform headers and footers, and employing grids and guides for aligned text and images. Typography played a crucial role, where selecting appropriate typefaces and mastering text formatting enhanced readability and aesthetic appeal. The overall design reflected the book's natural and organic theme, making it visually inviting.

Additionally, managing visual elements in InDesign was crucial for a visually rich book. Skills in image placement, resizing, and text wrapping were essential, as was effective use of layers for organization. A well-thought-out color palette set the book's mood, and understanding design principles like balance and contrast was key. These skills not only ensured a technically sound layout but also contributed to creating a book that was both informative and visually engaging.

Unique Features: Exclusively uses herbs and essential oils sourced from our family farm, ensuring the highest quality ingredients; combines a sense of Diaper and kindness, catering to parents who seek products that align with their values; offers a unique blend of organic herbs and essential oils for a gentle, soothing formula; eco-friendly and sustainably produced.

Target Audience: The ideal customer for Holler Creek Farm Diaper Balm is a parent who values organic, eco-friendly products for their baby's care, and who appreciates small-batch, artisanal goods. They likely have children in diapers or with sensitive skin and are seeking a gentle, effective, and environmentally responsible solution for diaper rash prevention and relief.

Value Proposition: Holler Creek Farm Organics offers a unique line of specialty organic products, prioritizing quality, sustainability, and eco-friendliness. Our Diaper Balm embodies these values and is specifically designed to provide effective and gentle care for your baby's delicate skin. Our commitment to using organically grown herbs and essential oils from our family farm ensures the highest quality ingredients in every product, making our offerings stand out among the competition.

Inspiration & Planning

Nestled in the heart of nature, Holler Creek Farm is a serene oasis that epitomizes the essence of organic living. This family-run farm, with its lush green fields and vibrant gardens, serves as a beacon of sustainability and natural wellness. The planning for Holler Creek Farm's branding drew heavily from its rustic charm and commitment to eco-friendly practices. Every element of the design was thoughtfully crafted to reflect the farm's dedication to organic farming and its deep connection with the natural world. The farm's tranquil environment and rich agricultural heritage were key inspirations in creating a brand that truly resonates with nature-loving individuals and families.

MerleFest

MerleFest is a celebrated annual music festival named in memory of Eddy Merle Watson, son of American folk music legend Doc Watson. Held in Wilkesboro, North Carolina, the festival is known for its unique blend of traditional, roots-oriented music, encompassing genres such as bluegrass, folk, Americana, country, and blues. MerleFest, founded in 1988, not only honors the musical legacy of the Watson family but also serves as a gathering point for music enthusiasts from all over. It features a diverse array of artists and has become one of the premier music festivals in the United States, renowned for its family-friendly atmosphere and a strong sense of community and tradition.

The yearly poster designs for MerleFest hold significant importance in advertising and symbolizing the festival. These posters are not just promotional tools; they are a blend of art and marketing that capture the spirit and essence of the event. Each year, the poster is carefully designed to reflect the festival's unique blend of music genres, its cultural significance, and the community it cultivates.
